# LEGAL SHIELD AI

AI Powered Cyber Crime Detection & Legal Assistant

---

## Overview

LEGAL SHIELD AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help users detect cyber threats and understand the legal steps required to report cybercrime.  

The system analyzes suspicious links, messages, or screenshots using artificial intelligence and provides legal guidance based on applicable cyber laws. It also assists users in generating structured complaint drafts that can be used for reporting cybercrime incidents.

---

## Problem

Cybercrime such as phishing attacks, online fraud, identity theft, and cyber harassment is rapidly increasing in the digital world.  

Many victims struggle to identify suspicious activities and often lack awareness about cybersecurity threats and the legal procedures required to report cybercrime.

Because of this gap between cybersecurity awareness and legal knowledge, many cybercrime cases go unreported.

---

## Solution

LEGAL SHIELD AI aims to bridge the gap between cybersecurity detection and legal assistance.  

The platform uses AI techniques to analyze suspicious digital content and provide users with information about potential threats and relevant cyber laws.

The system can also generate a structured cybercrime complaint draft based on the evidence provided by the user.

---

## Key Features

- AI-based scam and phishing detection  
- AI legal advisor for cybercrime guidance  
- Evidence analysis from links, messages, or screenshots  
- Automatic cybercrime complaint draft generation  
- Awareness about applicable cyber laws

## System Workflow

1. User uploads a suspicious link, message, or screenshot  
2. AI analyzes the content to detect potential threats  
3. The system identifies relevant cyber laws  
4. A structured complaint draft is generated  
5. The user receives legal guidance and recommended actions
